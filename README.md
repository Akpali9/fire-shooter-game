# 🔥 FIRE SHOOTER – Ultimate Online Shooting Game

![Fire Shooter Banner](https://via.placeholder.com/1200x400/0a0a0a/ff3300?text=FIRE+SHOOTER)

Fire Shooter is a high-octane, browser‑based multiplayer shooting game built with React, TypeScript, and Supabase. Jump into intense solo missions, team up with friends in co‑op, or compete in global PvP war rooms. Unlock powerful skills, climb the leaderboards, and become a legend.

## ✨ Features

### 🎮 Core Gameplay
- **Smooth WASD movement** + mouse‑aim shooting with real‑time hit feedback
- **4 stunning maps** – Industrial, Neon City, Nexus HQ, Warzone (each with unique visuals)
- **Intelligent enemy AI** – patrol, chase, shoot, and boss fights
- **Weapon mechanics** – ammo, reload, cooldown, muzzle flash, bullet tracers
- **Particle effects** – explosions, blood splatter, hit indicators

### 👥 Multiplayer & Social
- **Real‑time global chat** – talk to everyone in the lobby
- **War Rooms** – create or join public rooms for co‑op or competitive play
- **Team chat** – communicate with your squad during a match
- **Global leaderboard** – top players by kills and XP

### 💎 Progression & Monetisation
- **8 purchasable skills** – Rapid Fire, Armor, Stealth, Grenades, Medkit, Jetpack, Sniper, Berserker
- **Coin packages** – Stripe‑ready in‑app purchases
- **XP & levelling** – earn XP from missions, unlock higher difficulty
- **Profile stats** – kills, deaths, win/loss, total coins

### 🎯 Missions & Modes
- **Solo mode** – play any mission alone, fight waves + boss
- **Co‑op mode** – team up with friends in a shared room
- **Competitive mode** – race for the highest score in a public room
- **Time‑based missions** – complete before timer runs out

### 🛡️ Technical Excellence
- **Canvas‑based game engine** – smooth 60 FPS, no external physics engine
- **Supabase backend** – authentication, real‑time database, row‑level security
- **Responsive UI** – works on desktop (1440p down to 1024p)
- **No external game libraries** – pure TypeScript + Canvas API
